## ⚡ Animation Variety

**Multiple Animation Types:**
- `fadeSlideUp` - Slides up and fades in
- `fadeSlideLeft` - Slides from left
- `fadeSlideRight` - Slides from right
- `fadeScale` - Scales up smoothly
- `fadeRotate` - Rotates and scales
- `bounce` - Bouncing icons
- `glow` - Pulsing glow effect on borders
- `pulse` - Cards pulse on hover

**Section Animations:**
- Section 1: Slides up
- Section 2: Slides from left
- Section 3: Slides from right
- Section 4: Scales in
- Section 5: Rotates in
